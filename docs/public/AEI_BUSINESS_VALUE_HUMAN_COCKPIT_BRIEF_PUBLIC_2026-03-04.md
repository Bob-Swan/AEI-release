# AEI Business Value and Human Cockpit Brief (Public)

Date: March 4, 2026

## Executive Frame

AEI is a governance control plane for AI-assisted operations. It keeps automation observable, controllable, and auditable under real operating conditions.

## Why Governance Is the Product

AI can increase output, but reliability and trust boundaries determine whether teams can run safely at scale. Governance is the layer that captures upside while constraining downside risk.

## 3-Component Operating Model

1. Intent:
   - Human and AI systems propose actions.
2. Governor:
   - Policy evaluates each proposal.
3. Execution plus record:
   - Approved actions execute and are recorded.

Short form: `Propose -> Decide -> Execute`.

## Company Use Cases

1. Incident response:
   - Faster reconstruction and resolution through correlated timelines.
2. Regulated change control:
   - Clear policy enforcement before side effects.
3. Support handoff:
   - Standardized evidence exports for cross-team coordination.

## Human Cockpit MVP Blueprint

1. Status and policy gate panel:
   - Shows state and policy decisions.
2. Task queue and drilldown panel:
   - Shows lifecycle and execution context.
3. Event stream panel:
   - Shows real-time operational history and linkage.
4. Runbooks and quick actions panel:
   - Executes standard response motions with traceability.

## Acceptance Proof

1. Restricted action is blocked under policy with clear next-step guidance.
2. Task transitions are visible from queue to terminal state.
3. Correlated events are linked to task context.
4. Evidence export is available for handoff.

## Proof Metrics

1. MTTR improvement.
2. Time to first complete incident handoff.
3. Rate of blocked unauthorized actions.
4. Percentage of incidents with correlation-linked evidence.
